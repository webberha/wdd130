Content

Home page

If you dream of a ski vacation this winter but can’t afford it, consider one of these budget-friendly ski resorts. Many offer great runs, spectacular scenery, and even fewer crowds – without the high cost.

Images for the Home page

This no-frills mountain is in the Pacific Northwest of the State of Washington—Mt—Rainer Ski & Snow Park, located at the top of the iconic Mt. Adam.

Mt. Rainer provides epic skiing and snowboarding for all ages and abilities. With 60 runs, 8 lifts, an epic terrain park, a comprehensive ski school, and a friendly community, this unique place offers something for everyone.

Mt. Rainer prides itself on providing a relaxed, family atmosphere for skiers and riders of every ability!

Lesson and Rental

All ages, all levels, and most sizes (yes, even those size 16 ski boots for your 6'10" cousin)... we have you covered at the WP Rental and Learning Centers! We are here to help you build confidence. Regardless of your current level, we have got programs that guarantee success. Whether you are a first-timer, an expert skier, or a snowboarder, our staff can set you up with the right gear and technique to take you to the next level. We are happy to help you find the best lesson program and equipment for you or your family.

The Mountain

Discover a whole new playground at Mt. Rainier.

With majestic views of Mt. Adam, Mt. Rainier provides terrain for skiers and riders of every ability. Explore steeper terrain off the Great White Express, or explore Paradise Basin on the Couloir Express and Basin Quads. Our mid-mountain Lodge, The White Camp, is the perfect meeting point for brunch or an afternoon bbq and drinks. Mt. Rainier features slopeside lodging at the Snow Cap Inn, on-site Child Care, multiple dining options, full-service Learning, and Nordic, Rental & Retail Centers, all found in a relaxed family atmosphere. So leave the city behind and escape to Mt Rainier!
