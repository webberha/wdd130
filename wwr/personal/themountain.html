DISCOVER WHOLE NEW PLAYGROUND AT MT. RAINIER

With a majestic view of Mt. Adam, Mt. Rainier provides terrain for skiers and riders of every ability. Explore steeper terrain off the Express or Columbia Basin on the Express and Basin Quads. Our mid-mountain Lodge, The Sky Camp, is the perfect meeting point for brunch or an afternoon bbq and beer. Mt. Rainier features slopeside lodging at the White Inn, on-site Child Care, multiple dining options, full-service Learning, and Nordic, Rental & Retail Centers in a relaxed family atmosphere. So leave the city behind and escape to Mt. Rainier!
