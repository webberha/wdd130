Your Mountain and Your Happy Place

From beginners to experts, the mountain is home.

Welcome to Mt. Rainier Ski & Snow Haven, located at the top of the iconic Mt. Adam. Mt. Rainier provides epic skiing and snowboarding for all ages and abilities. With 60 runs, 8 lifts, an epic terrain park, a comprehensive ski school, and a friendly community, this unique place offers something for everyone

Slice of Heaven outside the city!

Experience a winter adventure at Mt. Rainier Ski and Snow Haven in majestic views of Mt. Adam while carving groomers in Snake Basin. Then, dive into steeps off the Ridge or explore Dovetail Peak's epic front side. Mt. Rainier prides itself on providing a relaxed, family atmosphere for skiers and riders of every ability!
